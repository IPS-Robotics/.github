# IPS Robotics Club ¯\\\_(ツ)\_/¯

IPS Robotics Club is a student-led and student-managed initiative started with one goal: **build cool things**.

We are a group of some of the most chaotic and nerdy students in the entire school. While the repositories of this organization may be a bit... chaotic, trust that we _are_ trying our best to maitain _some_ level of professionalism.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> We're not exactly paragons of code quality... Just keep that in mind when looking through our stuff.

## Club Projects
These projects are/were being developed together as a club, partly during regular meetings at school, partly during breaks between classes, and partly at home:
* `01` - **Line Follower**: as the name might suggest, it's a 2-wheel robot that can follow a line, or alternatively be remotely controlled.
